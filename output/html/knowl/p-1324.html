To test whether there is a linear relationship between \(x\) and \(y\text{:}\)
  • \(H_0: \beta_1 = 0\) (no linear relationship)
  • \(H_A: \beta_1 \neq 0\) (linear relationship exists)
Test statistic:
\begin{equation*} t = \frac{b_1 - 0}{SE_{b_1}} \end{equation*}
with degrees of freedom \(df = n - 2\text{,}\) where \(n\) is the number of observations.
