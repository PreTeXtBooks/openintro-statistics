Confidence Interval for \(\beta_1\).

A confidence interval for the true slope \(\beta_1\) can be constructed as:
\begin{equation*} b_1 \pm t^*_{df} \times SE_{b_1} \end{equation*}
where \(t^*_{df}\) is the critical value from the \(t\)-distribution with \(df = n - 2\) degrees of freedom.
