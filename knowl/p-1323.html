The standard error of the slope \(b_1\) is given by:
\begin{equation*} SE_{b_1} = \frac{s}{\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \bar{x})^2}} \end{equation*}
where \(s\) is the residual standard error:
\begin{equation*} s = \sqrt{\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} e_i^2}{n-2}} = \sqrt{\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2}{n-2}} \end{equation*}
