The coefficient of determination, denoted \(R^2\text{,}\) describes the proportion of variation in the response variable \(y\) that is explained by the least squares line with predictor \(x\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} R^2 = r^2 \end{equation*}
where \(r\) is the correlation between \(x\) and \(y\text{.}\)
