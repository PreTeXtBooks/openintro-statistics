When we fit a regression line to data, we are estimating the true population regression line:
\begin{equation*} y = \beta_0 + \beta_1 x + \varepsilon \end{equation*}
where \(\varepsilon \sim N(0, \sigma)\text{.}\) Our estimates \(b_0\) and \(b_1\) are point estimates of the true parameters \(\beta_0\) and \(\beta_1\text{.}\)
